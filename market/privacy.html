Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Red Barn Market LLC ("we", "us") values your privacy. This policy describes the information we collect and how we use it when you shop with us or sign up for updates.

Information we collect

  • Your name, delivery address, email, and phone number.
  • Order history and payment details (processed by our payment provider).
  • Your SMS opt-in status and a record of your consent.

SMS / Mobile Messaging Data

When you opt in to receive text messages from Red Barn Market, we collect your mobile phone number and a record of your consent (date, time, and the wording you agreed to). We use this information solely to send the messages you requested — weekly deals and order updates — and to support those messages. We do NOT sell, rent, or share your mobile opt-in information or phone number with third parties for their own marketing. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out anytime by replying STOP, or reply HELP for help.

How we use your information

We use your information to process orders, deliver products, send the updates you signed up for, and improve our service.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers (delivery, payment, and messaging providers) as needed to run the market, and never sell your personal data.
